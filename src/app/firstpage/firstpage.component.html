About us

Paragliding Resort is fulfilling an old dream of flying. We want to make paragliding popular as an adventure activity and sport as well as a tourist attraction in Jamaica. It is the simplest and most affordable way of flying and we believe that this sport is safer than its reputation makes us believe.
